Snellville, Georgia, is a city located in Gwinnett County, approximately 30 miles northeast of downtown Atlanta. What began as a small farming community in the 19th century has grown into a thriving suburban area, characterized by its family-friendly atmosphere and a mix of residential neighborhoods, commercial centers, and recreational amenities. The city prides itself on its community events, including an annual Snellville Days Festival, and offers a variety of parks and green spaces for residents to enjoy. Its proximity to major highways and the broader Atlanta metropolitan area provides convenient access to employment, entertainment, and educational opportunities, making Snellville a popular choice for those seeking a balance between suburban living and urban accessibility.
